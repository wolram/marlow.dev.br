The Journey

My Nomad Story

From a fixed office in Sao Paulo to building a life without borders. Here are the milestones that shaped my location-independent career.

2021

The Great Leap

Sao Paulo, Brazil

Negotiated full remote with my employer after proving productivity during the pandemic. Sold most possessions, kept two bags, and never looked back.

2022

European Chapter

Lisbon, Portugal

Landed in Lisbon with its thriving tech scene and nomad community. Managed global RPA projects while exploring castles on weekends.

2023

South American Return

Buenos Aires, Argentina

Discovered the magic of BA - incredible food, tango culture, and a cost of living that lets you live like royalty. Led my biggest automation project from a Palermo cafe.

2024

Multi-Hub Life

Madrid, Spain + Miami, USA

Established a rotation between continents. Madrid for its central timezone, Miami for US client meetings. The world became my office floor plan.

2025

Current Base

Porto, Portugal

Found my sweet spot in Porto - perfect weather, affordable living, amazing wine, and a timezone that works for both Americas and Europe. Writing this from a riverside co-working space.

Philosophy

Why Location Independence?

Remote work is not about escaping the office. It is about designing a life where work enhances living, not the other way around.

Async Excellence

When you work across time zones, you master async communication. Clear documentation, thoughtful messages, and respect for deep work time become second nature.

Fresh Perspectives

New environments spark creativity. The best solutions often come while walking through unfamiliar streets or working from a cafe in a new city.

Results Over Hours

I am measured by outcomes, not office hours. This accountability drives better work - when you own your time, you protect it fiercely.

Global Network

Nomad life builds an international network of collaborators, mentors, and friends. Every city adds new connections that enrich both work and life.

Intentional Living

Without a default routine, every choice is deliberate. Where to live, when to work, how to spend time - it all becomes purposeful.

Constant Growth

Adapting to new cultures, solving unexpected problems, and stepping outside comfort zones - nomad life is a masterclass in personal development.

Why Hire a Nomad?

I understand the hesitation. "Will they be available?" "Can we trust remote workers?" "What about time zones?" Here is why hiring location-independent professionals like me is actually a competitive advantage.

01

Proven Self-Management

Nomads who thrive have mastered discipline, time management, and accountability. We do not need supervision - we need clear goals and trust.

02

Timezone Flexibility

I design my location around work needs. Need overlap with US clients? I will be in the Americas. European focus? I am in Porto or Madrid.

03

Superior Documentation

Async work demands excellent documentation. Every process, decision, and piece of knowledge is captured - your team benefits permanently.

04

Results-Focused Mindset

Without office politics and commute stress, all energy goes to output. Judge me by deliverables, not hours logged.

05

Global Perspective

Working across cultures builds understanding of diverse markets, user needs, and communication styles. Invaluable for global products.

06

Always Reachable

Despite the locations, I maintain 99.9% availability during agreed hours. Backup internet, power banks, and contingency plans are standard.
